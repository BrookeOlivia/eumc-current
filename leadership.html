Reverend David Vaughan

Pastor David arrived at Evangelical in June of 2013. He is a graduate of the University of Virginia and Duke Divinity School, and he served as a youth minister, camp counselor, and hospital chaplain before becoming a pastor.


You're likely to find him reading Dr. Seuss, watching field of Dreams, cheering on the Virginia Cavaliers, or humming along with a Bruce Springsteen song, but most importantly he is the husband of April and father of Micah, a follower of Jesus, and an explorer searching for new ways to share God's love with the world.
